The Trello-For-Hill app provides custom interfaces for the Trello system used by the Hill-PCI team in managing projects. Click the menu button at the upper right to open the naviagion system and interact with the following tools:

  • Checklist to Cards

    Convert multiple checklist items to cards with links between the new cards and original checklist.

  • Card Printer

    Simple report allowing you to select any/all cards in a board and produce a printable .doc file.

  • Sprint Reporting

    Quick and easy sprint status report.
